Deathfire Grasp

Combat start: the holder fires a blast of energy at their target, marking them and dealing 20% of their maximum health as magic damage.

For the next 8 seconds, the holder's abilities and attacks deal 50% increased damage to the marked target, and 25% increased damage to other targets.
